Hoverbot

I modelled this little guy in Blender, and baked an ambient occlusion map out of the internal rendering engine and used that as a basis for the texture map. There's a subtle specular map to give the galvanized metal effect, and a REAL rough pass at some flames for an engine effect, but it got pretty late so I'm calling it!
